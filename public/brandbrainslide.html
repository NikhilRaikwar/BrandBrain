Solution + User Journey

One brain for ingest,
query, score, and share.

BrandBrain makes agency memory usable inside the workflow - from the first document to a scored rewrite.

📥
Step 1
Ingest
Paste campaigns, briefs, or brand guides into a client brain.
🧩
Step 2
Extract
GPT-4o-mini turns documents into structured knowledge cards and tags.
💬
Step 3
Query
Ask questions in plain English and get cited answers from the right brain.
⚡
Step 4
Score
Review copy against Ogilvy-style principles and the client's own history.
🔗
Step 5
Share
Send a public read-only link to clients or new hires.
